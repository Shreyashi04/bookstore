Pustak was built for curious minds who never stop learning. We curate the best e-books so you can focus on reading, not searching.

Our Story

Pustak started as a side project in 2022 by a group of developers and bookworms who were frustrated with cluttered e-book platforms. We wanted something clean, fast, and thoughtfully curated.

Today, we host over 12,000 titles across programming, business, fiction, and science — hand-reviewed by our editorial team before they ever reach your screen.

We believe reading is the highest ROI activity you can do. A single book can rewire how you think, solve a problem that's cost you months, or open a door you didn't know existed.
